Features that will help you build your capacity to become in demand,

The Empower blog

Discover new ideas tips & tricks, and get the wisdom to navigate your journey from school to work in the Empower blog.

The Leader's Forum

This feature will enable you create forums, invite your friends and mentors to engage in your forum, share experiences with your peers, ask questions & get them answered and enjoy the benefit of engaging with new ideas nationwide.

Gigs

The gigs feature will connect you to remote or local freelance jobs in the state of your residence that matches your skill set so you can some money for yourself while you look for a good job or build your business.

Training Directory

The Trainer/Training directory will help you discover good and affordable trainers or trainings happening near you. You will find trainings on a lot of skills you want to learn.

Opportunity Directory

The opportunity director will help you discover growth opportunities near you like competitions, scholarships, small business opportunities, grants, loan finance, and other such opportunities you can leverage.

Ventures Directory

This feature will enable you list your business idea or start-up venture and showcase it to potential investors, suppliers, partners, mentors and markets for your business.

Jobs

The Jobs directory will bring to you all the internship, apprenticeship & entry level positions you can take advantage of to build your value adding capacity.

Top

SAEDConnect exists to accelerate young graduates & students from school to rewarding work by connecting them to inspiration, knowledge, network, opportunities & support that can build their capacity to create value & start generating income for themselves.
