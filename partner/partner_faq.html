General

As a partner, what can I do on SAEDconnect?

Through SAED Connect, Partners can:

  • Register to become a SAED partner from anywhere in the globe.
  • Post trainings, events, SAED products, SAED Services, Impact programs and Self-help articles for corps members to access
  • Post proposals to offer strategic and program related intervention
  • Interact directly with any corps member who indicates interest in their intervention.
  • View all corps members who have applied for, or purchased their intervention.
  • Receive Payments made for their interventions from corps members nationwide through various channels
  • Respond to request for information/clarification from the SAED program managers on any of their interventions.
  • View announcements from their state or national SAED Office.
  • Provide Feedback to the SAED department

Are there fees for me to become a partner?

No there are no fees or charges for you to become a partner in the NYSC SAED program
