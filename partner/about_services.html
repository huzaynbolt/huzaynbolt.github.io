About support services

On their journey to self reliance through entrepreneurship and career excellence, corps members may require specialized expertise in crafting their career direction or in supporting a process navigating their journey.

We welcome service providers and consultants who offer expert help and support in areas like:

  • Career advisory, including interview prep, CV writing, career Counselling, etc.
  • Legal services,
  • Business development services,
  • Expert support in specific business areas
  • All other areas that might be useful to a corps Member in this discovery and growth phase of his journey.

We invite you to craft affordable packages within reach of the corps community and go ahead and engage with them through the SAED Program. With our Surety service, corps members who cannot immediately afford your services can gain quick loans to enable them access your services from our network of financial partners.

To offer support services to the corps member community, ensure you are registered as a partner. Then, login to access the SAED “Offering-Support-service” information template to guide you on how to submit a service package. Then follow the directions given to submit your service package to the SAED Admin for processing and kick-off.

Offering a Support service under the SAED Program

Partners who want to render support services to corps member will be required to provide the following basic information about any service they want to render.

1). About Support services.

In this section, you will be required to provide information about the service you want to render. Details to be provided include:

Details to be provided include:

  1. Service Title
  2. Service categories/ sub-categories
  3. Service description
    • What type of service are you rendering?
    • Goals/ objectives of the service( what is the benefit of this service to the corp member?)
    • Audience (who are those that the service can be rendered to?)
    • What qualifies a corp member to be a potential client?

2). Service package/Offers

Please provide the following information for the product offer.

  1. Offer Title
  2. Offer features (what does the corps member get under this offer?)
  3. Offer guarantees (What is your service quality guarantee to any corps member who patronizes you?)
  4. Offer Amount
  5. In what Location(s) is this offer Available?
  6. After payment, how do I access the service?
  7. Offer expiry date

3). Accreditation information

In this section, the information collected will be useful for the SAED administrator to assess your capacity to render these service. Please provide all the necessary information that will help us adequately assess your competence.

  1. What qualifies you to render this service?
