Program Description

In preparation for building the next generation entrepreneurs in Nigeria, we have provided a platform for serving corps members to access unsecured loans during their service year for varied purposes. Finance small businesses, gain a professional certification, buy commercial and lifestyle products and urgent cash needs. We have made it so easy and most convenient. Packages are suited to carter for the youth corps members in need of startup capital.

Features

  • No collateral or guarantor needed.
  • No extra Charges/ Fee (Management fees, processing fees and credit check fee).
  • Seamless application online: reduced documentation, validation of customer is done in partnership wth NYSC.
  • Repayment are equal throughout the facility life.
  • Free life and Hospitalization insurance cover throughout the duration of the loan.
  • 24 hours Turnaround Time.
  • Online processing ONLY.
