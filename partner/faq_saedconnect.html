What is SAEDConnect?

SAEDConnect is the online youth empowerment accelerator portal of the National Youth Service Corps Scheme that connects corps members nationwide to the trainings, products & services provided by partners. All partners who want to work with the NYSC SAED program are required to sign-up on SAEDConnect.

Why did the NYSC SAED program build SAEDConnect?

The NYSC SAED program built SAEDConnect to:

  1. Make it easier for partners globally to offer their interventions to the corps member community

  2. To make it easier for corps members to be able to access and engage with partners and their interventions from wherever they are posted to nationwide.

  3. To enable the SAED program managers better engage corps members to participate in the empowerment initiatives provided by partners.

  4. To enable the SAED program managers monitor the integrity of partner-corps member interaction and gather data to support advocacy drives.

What kind of partners can get on SAEDConnect?

Any partner who wants to do any of the below activities should register through SAEDConnect.

  1. Partners who want to submit a strategic/Program partnership proposal to submit the NYSC SAED program.

  2. Partners who want to offer an empowerment Training to corps members in a state or nationwide.

  3. Partners who want to host an Empowerment Events that corps members can benefit from.

  4. Partners who want to sell or lease an empowerment product (e.g vocational tools, books, raw materials, etc) that corps members can benefit from.

  5. Partners who want to provide an empowerment service (e.g legal services, business support services, mentorship services, work-spaces, skill practice centers etc) tp corps members

  6. Partners who want to run a business Incubation program for aspiring corps entrepreneurs

  7. Partners who want to offer finance to corps members including loans, grants & equity.

  8. Partners who have initiatives that can help corps entrepreneurs access markets to sell their products and services.

  9. Partners who want to host empowerment Competitions

  10. Partners who want to offer scholarships to exceptional corps members

  11. Partners who want to post job, Internship & apprenticeships opportunities.

  12. Partners who want to offer micro enterprise business opportunities and other opportunities that enable corps members generate income during their service year.

  13. Partners who want to offer any other type of youth empowerment program not explicitly stated above.

What can partners do on SAEDConnect?

Through SAEDConnect, partners globally can:

  1. Submit a strategic/program partnership proposal and get it treated by the SAED HQ.

  2. Showcase their trainings, events, products, services & empowerment programs to corps members nationwide. corps members will be able to access and purchase these interventions through the SAEDConnect platform.

  3. Engage with corps members who are interested in their interventions

  4. View all corps members who have applied for, or purchased their intervention

  5. Receive Payments made for their interventions from corps members nationwide through various channels and get instant notification.

  6. Respond to request for information/clarification from the SAED program managers on any of their interventions.

  7. View announcements from the state or national SAED Office.

  8. Provide Feedback to the SAED department

  9. Contribute self-help articles to the corps empowerment blog.

What is the benefit of SAEDConnect to partners?

SAEDConnect enables partners easily engage a homogenous group of over 300,000 educated youths across the 36 states of Nigeria including FCT annually, from wherever the partner is located. Depending on the type of partner, this means:

  • Lower cost of engagement and nationwide reach

  • Faster time to reach and impact youths with their empowerment trainings, events, products and services.

  • Wider market base of ready customers for their empowerment programs, products and services.

  • Endorsement of your empowerment initiative by the NYSC.

  • As SAEDConnect expands, give partners access to a wider audience for their interventions beyond the NYSC.

What can Corps members do on SAEDConnect?

Corps members nationwide can:

  • See and apply to participate in or purchase interventions from partners offering interventions near them.

  • Interact with this partners on their programs, products and services.

  • Make complaints directly to the SAED program managers.

  • Read empowerment articles posted on the SAED Empower blog.

  • Discuss in self-help forums
