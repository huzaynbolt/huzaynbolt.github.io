About SAED

Learn about the SAED program and the partnership principles of the program.

What is the NYSC SAED programme?

SAED (acronym for Skill Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development) is a nationwide initiative of the NYSC targeted at young Nigerian graduates mobilized and deployed in the 1-year mandatory service to empower them to become entrepreneurs or employable after their service year. The SAED program started in 2012.

How does the NYSC SAED programme achieve this mandate?

The SAED programme achieves result through 3 key activities

  1. Mindset reorientation: Helping these youths appreciate the reality of life after school & inspiring them to take positive action to become value generating.

  2. Right-skilling: Introducing these youths to in-demand entrepreneurship & employability skills and encouraging them to build value creating capacity in an area of their choice.

  3. Reducing the barrier to growth opportunities: by identifying these growth opportunities, negotiating easier access for these youths, and bringing them within easy reach.

How does the SAED program view partnerships?

The SAED programme is powered by partnerships. We achieve results working with credible individuals and organizations locally & internationally, who are interested in youth empowerment Social responsibility, or have solutions that could increase the effectiveness of the SAED program to deliver impact or who currently provide youth empowerment related products & services in line with the mandate of the programme.

What types of partnership is the NYSC SAED programme open to?

Individuals & organizations are invited to partner with the SAED program to deliver her mandate in any of three ways:

  1. As strategic partners: These type of partners support the SAED program at the infrastructural & policy influencing level to strengthen the program mandate, continuously ensure an enabling environment for the program to thrive & deepen the SAED program adoption amongst its audience. Learn more about Strategic partners and what they can do at www.nyscsaed.com/partner.

  2. As program partners: These type of partners provide tools & trainings to the program managers to execute on their mandate. Learn more about Program partners and what they can do at www.nyscsaed.com/partner.

  3. As Corps empowerment partners These type of partners provide youth empowerment interventions to the corps members that pass through the scheme annually either in a state or nationwide. Corps empowerment partners can offer a variety of interventions including:

    • Empowerment Trainings
    • Empowerment Events
    • Empowerment products (e.g vocational tools, books, raw materials, etc)
    • Empowerment services (e.g legal services, business support services, mentorship services, work-spaces, skill practice centers etc)
    • Business Incubation programs
    • Access to finance programs, including loans, grants & equity
    • Empowerment Competitions
    • Scholarships
    • Job opportunities, Internship & apprenticeships
    • Micro enterprise opportunities and other opportunities that enable corps members generate income during their service year.

    Learn more about Corps empowerment partners and what they can do at www.nyscsaed.com/partner.

What is the process to become a NYSC SAED strategic or program partner?

Three steps to becoming a NYSC SAED strategic or program partner,

  1. Sign up at partners.saedconnect.com

  2. Submit your strategic/program partnership proposal from your dashboard

  3. The SAED program managers will receive it and get back to you.

What is the process to become a NYSC SAED corps empowerment partner?

Three steps to becoming a NYSC SAED corps empowerment partner:

  1. Step 1: Sign up at partners.saedconnect.com

  2. Step 2: Apply on your dashboard to become verified and select the state where you will like to be verified.

  3. Step 3: Visit the SAED head in the state where you selected with relevant documents to back up your application. The details of the SAED head as contact address will be provided to you on your dashboard. As soon as a partner is verified, he/she can go on and start posting corps empowerment interventions.

Can I Charge a fee to offer my programs, products or services to corps members?

Yes, partners are allowed to charge corps members subsidized fees for their product, programs and services.

Do I pay to become a NYSC SAED Partner?

No, there is no fee attached to being a partner with NYSC SAED.
