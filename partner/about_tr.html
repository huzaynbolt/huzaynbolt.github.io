About Trainings & Events

The SAED Program welcomes credible individuals & organizations that offer quality trainings and high impact events in any of the following areas to bring their trainings & events to the corps member community.

  • Trainings/events in career development related subjects like identifying your core strength, crafting your career direction, etc.
  • Trainings/events in any of the 12 SAED Sector focus areas at beginner to advanced levels.
  • Trainings/events in employability development.
  • Trainings/events in technical Skills required to become competitive in different entry level job roles like Sales & Marketing, Business Analysis, etc.
  • Trainings/events in entrepreneurship development.
  • Trainings/events in accessing start-up and business growth finance.
  • Trainings/events in accessing local & international markets.

Whether you have the capacity to offer the training/event virtually, in one LGA, across a state, in multiple states or nationwide, you are welcome to offer them to the corps member community.

To post a training or event to the corps member community, ensure you are registered as a partner. Then, login to access the SAED training/event information template to guide you on the format to structure your training/event information. Then follow the directions given to submit your training/event to the SAEDConnect platform.

Your training/event will be assessed for its credibility & quality before it is posted to the Corp Member Community.

Applying to offer a Training/Event on the SAED Program

Partners who want to organize Skill Acquisition & Entrepreneurship development training and events for corps members will be required to provide the following information about such training or event.

1). About Training/Event

In this section, you will be required to provide basic information about what the event offers and what the corps members stand to gain by attending the event.

Details to be provided include:

  1. Event type:
    • Training
    • Seminar
    • Conference
    • Networking event
    • Other
  2. Training/Event categories and sub-categories.
  3. Training/Event Details
    • Title of Training/Event
    • Training/Event background (why is this training or event necessary?)
    • Goals/objectives of the training/event (what will corps members be able to do or how will corps members be better at the end of this training / event?)
    • Audience (Who is the training / event meant for?)
    • Training / Event organizer’s contact details for further enquiries (Tel No. , Email)

2). Training/Event Packages

In this section you will be required to provide information about your training/event schedule, venue and access fees (if any). If you will be hosting the training / event more than once, or in different locations, or if you have different fee packages, please post one package for each venue, schedule or fee level. You can post up to five (5) packages for a training/event.

Take note: You are required to post at least one training/event package before it can be published. Corps members will apply to attend your training/event by subscribing to a package.

Please provide the following information for your training/event package:

  1. Package title e.g. Early Bird Registration, Virtual Session or Lagos Stream 1.
  2. Max No. of Available Seats.
  3. Start date.
  4. Duration.
  5. Days and times for training/ event (where applicable).
  6. Venue.
    • Address
    • Local government
    • State
  7. Package Fee(amount):
    • Amount
    • Free (Select “Free” if you are not charging any fees).
  8. What will the corps Member get under this package? (E.g. materials,Souvenir(s), lunch, certificates etc. and what is excluded, which they will have to pay for separately?) Take note, if a corps Member reports that you charge an extra fee for any thing not stated here, you will be penalized as a partner.
  9. Expiry date of this package/Deadline for application to this package: this is the day when this package would cease to be available to corps members

3). Accreditation information

Information in this section will be useful for the SAED administrator to assess your capacity to organize this training or event smoothly. Please provide all the necessary information that will help us adequately assess your competence.

  1. Have you organized this training or event before?
  2. What qualifies you to organize this training or event?
  3. Where applicable, what special resources/tools do you possess that will enable you adequately organize this event?
