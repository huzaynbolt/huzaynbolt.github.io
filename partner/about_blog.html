About Empower Blog

Do you already write on topics related to career development, entrepreneurship development, personal development or skill development in any area?

We offer a brand new audience of over 300,000 youths who can be impacted by your thoughts. We invite you to contribute to the NYSC SAED Empower blog.

Posting articles on SAED connect is quite easy. All you have to do is get registered as a partner and then go ahead to post your article.

Articles are vetted by our team of editors before they are published to the Empower Blog.
