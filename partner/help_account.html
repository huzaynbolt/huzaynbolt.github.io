Account Setup

How do I change my password?

To change your password, here’s what you have to do.

  • On your Dashboard page, click on the “My Profile Setting” on the main menu to the right.
  • Click on the “Manage Password” link on the left (contextual) link.
  • Fill in the form field appropriately and click on the “Change Password” link. A message stating “Success, Password Successfully Changed” will appear. Click on the “Ok” button to close the dialog.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

Here’s what you have to do if you forgot your password:

  • If you are still logged in, Follow the directions given on "How to change your password".
  • If you are not logged in, go to the login page, and find the “forgot password link”. You will be taken to another page. Which is the “Forgot Password” page.
  • Provide your login email address in the form field.
  • Click on the “Send Reset ” button. To confirm that you want to change your password.
  • An email will be sent to the email address you provided with a link to reset your password. Note, You will only receive this email if it is the same email address you used to register on SAEDConnect.
  • Find this email in your email inbox, and click on the “Email Reset Link” that has been sent to you to load a page where you can change your password.
  • Log in with your new password to continue using your SAEDConnect partner account.

How do I edit my contact information?

To edit your contact information, here’s what you have to do:

  • Click on the “My Profile Setting” link on the main menu list to the right of the page.
  • Click on the “Contact Information” link on the left hand side below your profile picture.
  • Click on the black “Edit” button at the top the page to display the edit form.
  • Fill your contact information in the fields provided, Click the “Save Changes” button to save your details.

How do I edit my bio?

To edit your bio, here’s what you have to do:

  • Click on the “My Profile Setting” link on the main menu list to the right of the page.
  • Click on the “Bio” link.
  • Click on the “Edit” button.
  • Edit your bio appropriately and click on the “Save Changes” button. You will see the message SUCCESS, your profile has been updated.
