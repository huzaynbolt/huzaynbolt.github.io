About Product offers/sales/lease

Micro enterprise opportunities like distribution franchises and other geographical coverage related opportunities are a great way for corps members to earn legitimate income, while getting introduced to the basics of entrepreneurship.

The SAED Program welcomes partners who see the nationwide network and undiluted energy of the corps members as a veritable platform to deepen their distribution network to engage with us to structure mutually beneficial micro enterprises for interested corps members.

Every form of legitimate micro enterprise opportunity that will not distract the corps members from their primary assignment is welcome.

With our Surety service, corps members can gain quick loans to jump start their participation in your opportunity from our network of financial partners.

Our Micro enterprise program will train corps members on the basics of running a small business and subsequently expose them to various opportunities to choose from.

To offer a micro enterprise opportunity to the corps community, please register as a partner on SAEDConnect and the. Go ahead and send us information of your opportunity by filling the micro-enterprise opportunity form. We will then review your opportunity and work out modalities to get corps members involved.

Format for posting a product/tool/equipment for sale or lease to SAEDConnect

Partners who have educational products, vocational tools, workspaces and any other form of tangible product which can support a corps member drive towards entrepreneurship or employability and want to lease or sell these products/resources to corps members will be required to provide the following information about such products.

1). About Product

In this section, you will be required to provide basic information about the product you want to offer the corps community

Details to be provided include:

  1. Product categories/ sub-categories
  2. Product Details
    • Product Name
    • Product Type (Book or educational material, Vocational Tool/Equipment, Physical Space, Others).
    • Product description
    • Product Specification
    • Product condition: (Used, new)
    • Contact details for tools and resource providers for further enquires

2). Product Packages/Offers

Please provide the following information for the product offer.

  1. Offer Title
  2. Offer type (Sale, Long Lease, short lease, staggered)
  3. Offer Amount
  4. Offer Terms (what are the conditions for accessing these offer?)
  5. In what Location(s) is this offer Available?
  6. Delivery information across all locations of availability: After payment, how do I access the product?
  7. Offer expiry date
  8. Product Return/Refusal Policy
