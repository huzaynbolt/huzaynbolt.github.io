How do I get Started as a partner on SAEDConnect?

To get started on SAEDConnect, visit http://partners.saedconnect.com and sign up. We recommend you use the Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox browser or Safari browser (on the Mac) to access SAEDConnect.

I Have several branches of my company round the country. Does each branch need to sign up as a SAED partner?

No. A partner organization is only required to sign-up once even if he/she has branches in more than one state. E.g, if your training outfit has branches in Lagos, Abuja & Port Harcourt, you do not need to sign up with 3 different accounts. As long as it’s one company, all branches are supposed to be managed under the same account, as, to the NYSC SAED program, all your branches are still one partner. We have however incorporated features for you to create multiple users under your company account so that each user can create and manage the interventions from their branch.

What Happens during the sign-up phase?

To Sign-up, you will be required to create a partner account. At the sign-up page, you will need the following:

  1. An email address: We recommend you the official Email Address of the company applying. If you are applying on behalf of your organization, please enquire from them what email address to use. All official communication from NYSC SAED to partners will be made through this email. Also, please ensure that you have access to these email address as you will be required to verify the authenticity of the email.

  2. Your partner account password.

Once you are done completing the Sign-up form, a verification email will be sent to your email. You will be required to open the email and follow the instructions to verify the authenticity of your email address. Once you do this, you will be directed to the partner application form. If you do not receive the email, please check your spam. If the email still isn’t in your spam, please click the resend email link on your sign-up page. If this still doesn’t solve the matter, kindly contact partners@saedconnect.com for support.

What Happens after I verify my email address?

Once you have verified your email, you will be directed to a 4 part application form, which you will be required to complete. The form will enable you provide the SAED program necessary information about who you are, what you do, how to contact you, and your interest in the SAED program.

After completing the Application form & submitting, what happens next?

Once you are done with the partner application process, you will be directed to a dashboard. At this stage, you can go ahead and do any of the following:

  1. Submit a strategic/partner proposal to the SAED program. You will see a link to do this on your dashboard.

  2. Post a self-help article to the empower Blog for corps members to read. You will see a link to do this on your dashboard.

  3. Create new users under your account.

  4. Update Create new branch addresses and contact details under your profile settings.

  5. Update your bank account information, where you intend to offer premium interventions that corps members will be required to pay for. You are able to put in more than one bank account detail. You will be able to do this under your profile setting as well.

If however you want to post a training, event, product, service e.t.c that corps members will be able to see and apply to, you will need to apply for verification of your partner Application details. This process is required to ensure that only credible partners are allowed to post interventions that corps members can apply for.

How do I apply to be verified as a credible partner?

The process of partner verification entails a SAED officer in any state of your choice seeing you or your representative physically and ascertaining that the information your provided on your application form is authentic. Here is how to schedule your partner verification.

  1. On your partner dashboard, you will see a “Verify” Button. Click it.

  2. You will be requested to select the state where you want to be verified.

  3. After you select your state of choice for verification and submit, you will see a confirmation message that will provide the name of the SAED officer in the state of your choice, address of the SAED office, email address of the SAED officer in that state, as well as partner visiting day. You will also receive a notification email confirming that you have scheduled a partner verification.

  4. You will then be required to visit the SAED office in that state on the partner visiting day with all relevant documents to prove that you are credible. The SAED officer may request a further visit to your office where the need arises.

  5. Once the SAED officer is satisfied, he/she will then go ahead to confirm you.
    Take note that you only need to be verified once, even if you have branches in other states.

What Happens after you are verified?

As soon as you are confirmed by the SAED officer as a credible partner, you will be able to post any type of intervention for the corps members, as provided by the SAEDConnect Platform.

How do I post an intervention?

To post an intervention, click the relevant intervention type link on your dashboard or from the main menu to the left of your dashboard and follow the steps.
