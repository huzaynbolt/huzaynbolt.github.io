Frequently Asked Questions

Is the SAED program open to non-corps members?

No. For now, the SAED program is exclusively for corps members.

Do I pay to register on the SAED program?

No. registering on the SAED program is free.

What else do I have to do to benefit from the SAED program?

You only have to register to start benefiting from the SAED program. However, to access some opportunities in the program, you might need to fulfil some requirements.

What happens after I pass out?

The SAED program is built to empower you in such a way that you will be able to stand on your own by the time you pass out.
